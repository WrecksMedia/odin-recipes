Bacon-Wrapped Jalapeño Poppers

Jalapeño Poppers: A Spicy Appetizer Delight

Turn up the heat with these irresistible Jalapeño Poppers. Creamy, cheesy filling meets the spicy kick of fresh jalapeños, all wrapped in a crunchy, golden coating. Perfect as a crowd-pleasing appetizer or game-day snack.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup cream cheese
  • ½ cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 12 jalapeño peppers, halved lengthwise, seeds and membranes removed
  • 12 slices bacon

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Mix cream cheese and Cheddar cheese together in a bowl until evenly blended. Fill each jalapeño half with cheese mixture.
  3. Put halves back together and wrap each stuffed pepper with a slice of bacon. Arrange bacon-wrapped jalapeño peppers on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until bacon is crispy, 25 to 35 minutes.
  5. Serve poppers hot with your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy!

Nutritional Facts

  • 265 calories
  • 21g fat
  • 5g carbs
  • 14g protein
